We currently offer the following bookkeping services

General Bookkeeping: Bookkeepers manage day-to-day financial transactions, such as recording sales, expenses, and payments, ensuring accuracy and completeness of financial records.

Accounts Payable Management: Handle vendor invoices, track due dates, and process payments to suppliers, ensuring timely payments while maintaining accurate records of payables.

Accounts Receivable Management: Track customer invoices, manage billing processes, and monitor collections to ensure timely receipt of payments, improving cash flow and reducing outstanding receivables.

Bank Reconciliation: Reconcile bank statements with the company's financial records, identifying discrepancies, errors, and fraudulent activities, ensuring the accuracy of the financial statements.

Financial Reporting: Prepare financial reports, including income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, and other customized reports, providing a clear overview of the company's financial performance.

Payroll Processing: Calculate employee wages, deduct applicable taxes, file statatory deductions on taxes to avoid government penalties and interest prepare pay stubs, and ensure compliance with payroll regulations, providing accurate and timely payroll processing services.

Tax Preparation: Organize financial records, categorize transactions, and collaborate with accountants to prepare and file tax returns, ensuring compliance with tax laws and maximizing deductions.

Expense Tracking and Analysis: Monitor and analyze business expenses, categorize them, and provide detailed expense reports, helping businesses identify cost-saving opportunities and improve profitability.

Financial Software Management: Assist in setting up and managing accounting software, such as QuickBooks, ensuring proper data entry, customization, and generating accurate financial reports.

Financial Consultation: Provide financial insights and recommendations based on analyzing financial data, helping businesses make informed decisions, optimize cash flow, and improve financial performance.
